## Responsibilities Summary

- **Team Manager:** Oversees the QA strategy, coordinates schedules, and actively participates in testing  
- **Risk Analyst:** Identifies high-risk areas, creates rigorous test cases, ensures critical scenarios are tested  
- **Test Executor:** Runs assigned test cases, logs results, reports defects, and verifies fixes  
- **Documentation Owner:** Consolidates findings, updates markdown documentation, ensures clarity and completeness  
- **Jira Admin:** Maintains issue tracking, labels, dashboards, and links to feature requirements  
- **Communications Coordinator:** Keeps the team informed, facilitates updates, and ensures smooth collaboration
